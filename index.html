OUR MENU

We offer a diverse and flavorful menu featuring authentic dishes made with fresh ingredients and traditional spices. Start with appetizers like crispy fried spring rolls, refreshing summer rolls, or tempura scampi. Main courses include hearty noodle dishes such as Bun Cha Ha Noi with grilled pork, Bun Bo Hue with slow-cooked meats, and the crispy Banh Xeo pancake filled with scampi and pork. For a quick yet delicious option, try the classic Bánh Mì baguettes with chicken, beef, or omelet, all served with vibrant pickled vegetables and house-made sauces.
